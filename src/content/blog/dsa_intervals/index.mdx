## Introduction
Intervals are a fundamental data structure in computer science, representing a range of values between a start and an end point.
They are widely used in various algorithms and applications, such as scheduling, merging overlapping intervals,
and finding the maximum number of overlapping intervals at any given time.

In this blog post, we will explore a collection of problems related to intervals,
covering various techniques and approaches to solve them efficiently.
